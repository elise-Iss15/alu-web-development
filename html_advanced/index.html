Frequently Asked Questions

What are the requirements to enroll?

You need to be at least 18 years old and provide a valid ID to enroll in our courses.

How long do the courses take?

Course duration varies, but most beginner courses last 4-6 weeks with flexible schedules.

Do you offer weekend classes?

Yes, we offer both weekend and evening classes to accommodate your schedule.

Is there support for the driving license process?

Absolutely! We guide you through every step of the driving license application process.
